Tiān Guān Cì Fú | 天官赐福

A Chinese web novel written by Mo Xiang Tong Xiu. It was first published in 2017. On August 4th, 2021, the official English translation and physical copy were announced by Seven Seas Entertainment. The English version will be split into 8 volumes with the final one releasing in March 2023.
